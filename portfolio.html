Hi Klas Fashion

Hi Klas Fashion is an fashion brand without an online presence. During the pandemic, I as given the task of creating an online presence for them in order to generate sales, whilst their store was closed.

Grade Calculator

The idea for this project came, when I found it difficult to track and understand the progress I was making in Uni. So I created an app to do just that. You enter your modules and current grades, and it provides you with feedback, so you can better understand the progress you are making for the semester.
The languages used in this project were HTML5, CSS & Javascript.
